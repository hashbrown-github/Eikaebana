Refund Policy

Last updated date: June 20, 2022

General

a. This website with the URL of https://shop.eikaebana.com ("Website/Site")is operated by Eikaebana Flower Shop ("We/Our/Us").

b. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products. However, on rare occasions, products may be found to be faulty or deficient. In such cases, we offer return and refund facilities in accordance with this Refund Policy ("Policy").

c. You are advised to read our Terms and Conditions along with this Policy at the following webpage: https://shop.eikaebana.com/terms-and-conditions.

d. By using this website, you agree to be bound by the terms contained in this Policy without modification. If you do not agree to the terms contained in this Policy, you are advised not to transact on this website.

e. We offer a 7-day refund Policy for eligible products.

f. Please read this Policy before making a purchase on this Website so that you understand your rights as well as what you can expect from us if you are not happy with your purchase.

Definitions

a. "Business Days" - means a day that is not a Saturday, Sunday, public holiday, or bank holiday in India or in the state where our office is located.

b. "Customer" - means a person who buys any goods or avails services for consideration but does not include a person who purchases such goods or avails services to resell them.

c.  "Date of Transaction"- means the date when a customer buys any goods or avails services for consideration but does not include a person who purchases such goods or avails services to resell them.

d."Website" - means this website with the URL:  https://shop.eikaebana.com.

Refunds Rules

a. Every effort is made to service the orders placed as per the specifications and timelines mentioned with respect to the products. If due to any unforeseen circumstances or limitations from our side, the order is not shipped or delivered then such order stands cancelled, and the amount paid by you is refunded.

b. We will not process a return if you have placed the order for the wrong product model, colour, or incorrect product or service.

c. When you make a qualifying return, we will refund the full amount, less the original shipping and handling charges.

d. Return or refund shall only be considered once the customer concerned produces relevant documents and proof.

e. Return and refund shall be subject to testing and verifying the alleged fault by an authorized person on behalf of us.

f. The product will be eligible for replacement only if the same product/model is available in stock. In case the same product/model is out of stock, a refund shall be provided.

g. Once qualified, the refunds are applied to the original payment option.

h. Please be informed that products purchased using the Cash on Delivery option will be refunded to the bank account with the details provided by you to us.

i. Customers who wish to make a warranty claim must comply with the manufacturer's instructions and warranty procedure. If the details of the product manufacturer are not available, we will help you with such details to directly connect with the manufacturer to resolve your issues.

j. Please note that digital products are non-returnable unless such digital product is materially non-compliant with its specifications or the physical media on which it is supplied is defective.

Products Damaged During Transit

a. Replacement can be made if the customer establishes that the product was delivered in defective condition or has physical damage within 12 hours of receipt of the product.

b. We will organize to repair the damaged product or collect it and replace it with an equivalent product, or provide a refund, provided that you contact us within 12 hours of receipt of the product.

c. Any damaged product must be returned in the condition it was in when you received it, together with any packaging and other items which you received with the damaged product.

Order Not Confirmed but Amount Deducted

a. If the amount has been deducted and the order is not confirmed, please do contact your respective bank. It takes 7 (seven) business days to reverse back the amount by the respective bank.

b. If the issue has not been resolved within 7 (seven) business days, you can contact our customer care support as follows: shop@eikaebana.com .

Exemptions

a. Notwithstanding the other provisions of this Policy, we may refuse to provide repair, replacement, or refund for a product or service you purchased if:

I. The product purchased is sale items or promotional items (discounts, giveaways, etc.).

II. If the product has been used or opened.

III. The products are not in the same condition as when they were delivered.

IV. Misused the product in a way that caused the problem.

V. You knew or were made aware of the problem(s) with the product or service before you purchased it.

VI. Gifts or free services.

VII. The product is broken, tampered with, or damaged.

VIII. The product is returned after the return window is closed or the Refund Period expires.

IX. Any other exceptions apply under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.

b. Apart from the aforementioned goods and services, the following goods and services are not eligible for a refund: Artificial Flowers

Rules of Returning the Product

a. The request for cancellation of products can be made in the following manner:

Please send an email to shop@eikaebana.com along with order details within 12 hours of receiving your order.

b. You are eligible to return the product only if you return it in the same condition in which you received it.

c. Pack the product properly and label it with your order number, return address, and your address.

d. The product shall be returned in its original packaging.

e. We do not accept liability for packages damaged during return transit; it is your responsibility to pack it properly to prevent any damage during transit.

f. Our technical support staff or customer support staff will advise you on which method of delivery to use to return your products; authorized product returns must be sent to the address communicated by our authorized personnel.

g. The order returned must accompany all accessories (including freebies) which were serviced along with your order; otherwise, we shall recover their cost from your refund amount.

h. On receipt of your returned product, we will test it to identify any faults you have notified us about.

i. A copy of your receipt or invoice shall be attached along with your returned product.

j. You will receive an email or SMS notification at your email address or mobile number provided to us.

Shipping Cost

a. We provide all support to return products; however, any cost incurred on packaging or return shall be borne by you.

b. If you are sending back a product, the risk of loss lies with you; proof of postage is not proof of delivery; therefore, you are strongly advised to send your package by recorded delivery, registered post, or courier.

9. PRODUCTS CONTAINING YOUR DATA

a. In some cases, your data may be stored in devices you are returning to us; it is advised that you take a backup and remove all your data before returning any products; we are not responsible for any loss or misuse of your data.

b. The privacy of your data supplied during return and refund procedures is also governed by our privacy policy, which can be accessed under this link: https://shop.eikaebana.com/privacy-policy .

Response Time

a. Refunds are normally processed within 12 business days after completing quality checks on returned products.

b. Refunds are normally processed within 12 business days after checking the veracity of refund requests.

c. The period for refunds may also depend on various banking and payment channels; we will not be liable for any errors or delays due to banks or third-party service providers.

Cancellation of Return Request

A request for return or refund once made can be cancelled by contacting customer care at shop@eikaebana.com.

Refusal of Return or Refund Request

We reserve the right to refuse or cancel any return request if such request does not comply with this Policy or applicable laws.

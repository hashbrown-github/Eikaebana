About Us

While the joy of natural flowers and plants fades away with changing seasons, artificial flowers are a permanent source of beauty. They don’t restrict you to the seasonal availability to spruce up your office space or personal room.

We are one of the largest artificial flower wholesale companies in the country with a trade catalog of four hundred unique products. We specialize in the production and import-export of artificial flowers, plants, stems, and Christmas ornaments.

We ventured into this field back in 2001. Over the last twenty years we have closely watched and understood our customers' tastes and preferences. Ultimately, it has helped us to enhance our selection process and serve our clients effectively.

Quality has always been our primary focus. We maintain our exclusive rights by manufacturing bespoke products in factories that meet the highest quality standards and generates higher customer satisfaction. We believe in constantly revising our product mix to ensure better designs and to stay fresh.

Our exquisite collection of flowers ranges from fascinating rose buds and vibrating shades of orchids to wall hanging flowers. Furthermore, our wide collection of products also includes floral backgrounds and backdrops, flower vases and pots, artificial grass mats, artificial flower stems and sticks, floral cutters, Christmas trees, ornaments and much more.

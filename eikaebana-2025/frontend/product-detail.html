  • Avoid Direct Sunlight

    To prevent fading, keep artificial plants out of direct sunlight. This will help maintain their color and appearance over time.

  • Dust Regularly

    Take your artificial flowers outdoors and gently dust them with a damp cloth or soft feather duster to remove surface dust and debris.

  • Use a Hairdryer or Compressed Air

    For intricate areas, use a hairdryer on a cool setting or compressed air to dislodge dust without damaging the flowers. Keep the hairdryer at a distance while sweeping across the arrangement.

  • Deep Clean with Soap and Water

    For fabric and plastic combinations, mix mild soap with water and dampen a cloth or sponge. Gently wipe the flowers, focusing on stained areas, and avoid saturating them. Allow the flowers to air dry completely after cleaning.
